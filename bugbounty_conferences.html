Why Attend Bug Bounty Conferences?

Bug bounty conferences are essential for staying up-to-date with the latest vulnerabilities, techniques, tools, and real-world experiences shared by top ethical hackers. These events provide unmatched networking opportunities, hands-on workshops, and access to private programs or partnerships.

  1. Plan Ahead: Review the speaker lineup and choose sessions aligned with your interests (e.g. XSS, SSRF, mobile bugs).
  2. Join Early: For popular events, join the livestream or venue early to secure your spot.

  4. Take Notes: Document takeaways, tools mentioned, and tips from each session.
  5. Network: Introduce yourself to fellow hunters, sponsors, and platform reps.
