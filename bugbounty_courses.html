Why Learn Bug Bounty Hunting?

Bug bounty hunting is the modern battlefield of ethical hackers. Companies like Google, Facebook, and Apple reward security researchers for responsibly disclosing vulnerabilities in their platforms. If you're looking to break into cybersecurity or earn income while learning, this is one of the most exciting paths to explore.

üß≠ Learning Path

Beginner Level

  • Introduction to Web Security: Understand HTTP, HTTPS, and how websites work.
  • Basic Reconnaissance: Learn subdomain enumeration, directory fuzzing, and whois lookups.
  • Understanding OWASP Top 10: Focus on Input Validation, XSS, and Broken Authentication.
  • Tools: Burp Suite Community, Nmap, Google Dorks, theHarvester

Intermediate Level

  • Advanced Recon & Automation: APIs, Shodan, scripting with Python/Bash
  • In-Depth Vulnerabilities: IDOR, SSRF, Business Logic, JWT manipulation
  • Using Burp Suite Pro: Interception, Repeater, Intruder, Collaborator
  • Reporting Best Practices: CVSS scoring, clear PoCs, reproducible steps

Advanced Level

  • Hunting on Real Platforms: HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Intigriti
  • Targeting Complex Applications: OAuth issues, GraphQL flaws, multi-tenant bugs
  • Chaining Vulnerabilities: Finding critical impact through chaining low severity bugs
  • Bug Bounty Automation: Use tools like ffuf, nuclei, Interlace
